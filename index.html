Sixthsense

Sixthsense is a open-for-all general quiz competition with no age limit as such. Teaming format is duo or lone wolves, and team-up process is flexible and easy.

Convergence

Convergence is a mainly bizTech genre quiz, with added spices of sports. The duo team members or lone wolves must be under 25 to participate.

Fandomania

Fandomania is a quiz based on only fandom and Pop culture. A goldmine for Pop culture fans, The duo team or lone wolves participants must be under 25.

Hit the Homerun

Hit the Homerun is a sports and games quiz, specially made with care for the sports quizzers out there. The duo team members or lone wolves participating must be under the age of 25.

Jana Ojana

Jana Ojana is a general school quiz. School students currently in classes 8 to 12 are allowed to participate in a team of 2 or lone wolf format.
